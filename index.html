What is BMI? How does it works?

The Body Mass Index (BMI for short) is the ratio of a person's weight to the square of their height that estimates their body fat. It's widely used to indicate whether the person has a healthy body weight for their height or not. The value obtained from the BMI calculation is categorized as: underweight, normal weight, overweight or obese, depending on the range the value falls between, changing according to the person's age or ethnicity.
